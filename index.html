About Alchemy Partners

Where Technical Rigor Meets Social Inclusion

Alchemy Partners bridges the gap between technical expertise and inclusive development. Our approach transforms complex governance challenges into actionable strategies that center equity, evidence, and impact.

Founded by Angelique Shika Akpaloo, a chemical engineer turned governance specialist, we bring a unique perspective to energy transition, extractive sector reform, and gender-responsive policy design.

We've partnered with leading organizations including EITI International, GIZ, World Bank, and UN agencies to deliver high-impact projects across Ghana and internationally.
